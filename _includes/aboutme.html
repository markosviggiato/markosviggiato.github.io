About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher at the Analytics of Software, GAmes And Repository Data (ASGAARD) lab, at the University of Alberta. I received my M.Sc. degree in Computer Science and the B.E. degree in Control and Automation Engineering from the Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG), Brazil.
I am extremely enthusiastic about how data analytics and AI can play an important role in different fields, such as games: from game design to gameplay to game data analysis. I am eager to advance and apply data analytics and AI techniques to support game creators and players. I am also interested in helping people from other industry domains with my analytics skills.
